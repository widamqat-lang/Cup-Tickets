📋 Booking Steps

1

Choose Your Match

Browse the 72 matches schedule and choose the match you want to attend. Each match shows date, stadium, and teams.

2

Select Your Seat

Use the interactive map to choose your preferred seat. Green seats are available, red are reserved. See each seat's price in real-time.

3

Enter Your Details

Fill out the simple form: Full name, WhatsApp number (for communication), and email (optional).

4

Confirm Your Booking

We'll contact you via WhatsApp within 24 hours to confirm your booking and complete payment. Tickets will be sent after confirmation.

How long does confirmation take?

Usually we confirm within 2-24 hours. During peak times it may take up to 2 days.

Yes, all tickets are authentic from FIFA. We work as a trusted intermediary with years of experience.

Cancellation policy depends on timing. Contact us for details.

How do I receive my ticket?

Tickets are sent via email or WhatsApp after payment confirmation. You can print them or show them on your phone.
