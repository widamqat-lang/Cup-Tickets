❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What's the cheapest ticket?

The cheapest ticket is only $25 for Section D. This competitive price makes attending the World Cup accessible to everyone.

Are prices the same for all matches?

Yes, the same prices apply to all 72 matches from group stage to the final.

What's the difference between sections?

The main difference is the location in the stadium - sections close to the field (A, B) are more expensive due to proximity to the action.

Can I choose a specific seat?

Yes! Through our interactive map you can choose any available seat and see its price in real-time.

Are prices in USD?

Yes, all prices are in US Dollars. Payment can be made via bank transfer or WhatsApp.
